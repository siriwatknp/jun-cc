This skill enhances your ability to work with MUI ecosystem better. It asssumes you already have good foundation knowledge of MUI products and components.

Depending on the specific MUI packages involved in the user's request, apply relevant skills as needed:

- For theme and styling customizations, take [MUI Theming and Styling skill](./mui-theming-styling.md) into account.
- For `@mui/material`, take [Material UI skill](./material-ui.md) into account.
- For `@mui/x-charts`, take [Charts skill](./x-charts.md) into account.
